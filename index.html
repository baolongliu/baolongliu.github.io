I received my PhD in Computer Science and Technology from Zhejiang University in 2018. Afterward, I join Alibaba Group as an algorithm expert specializing in artificial intelligence. In 2021, I begin working at the School of Computer Science and Technology, Zhejiang Gongshang University. My research focuses on human-computer interaction, intelligent transportation, video understanding, and LLMs, aiming to bridge scientific research with practical applications. I lead projects funded by the National Natural Science Foundation of China and the Zhejiang Provincial Natural Science Foundation. I publish numerous papers in major international conferences like ICCV, AAAI, ACM SIGIR, and ACM MM.
